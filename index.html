Who's Happy?

We use the Cantril Ladder from the Gallup World Poll to measure happiness. Respondents in the poll are asked to think of a ladder, where 0 is the worst life they could possibly have and 10 is the best. They are then asked to rate their own lives on this scale. The mean is then taken for each country.
